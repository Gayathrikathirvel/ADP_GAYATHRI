The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, established in 1985, is renowned for its exceptional teaching and research, supported by a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculty members. Dr.K.Muthulakshmi’s academic journey reflects her commitment to advancing her expertise in Electronics and Communication Engineering. As Professor and Head of the department, her leadership is evident in her ability to guide and inspire students, faculty, and research teams.

Under her leadership, the department has sustained its reputation for excellence in both teaching and research, thanks to her vision, guidance, and dedication to creating a supportive and innovative environment. Dr. K.Muthulakshmi began her academic career by earning a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Periyar Maniammai College of Technology for Women, Thanjavur, in 1999, which laid the foundation for her scholarly pursuits. She further developed her expertise with a Master’s degree in Computer and Communication Engineering from Tamil Nadu College of Engineering, Coimbatore, in 2008. In 2016, Dr. K.Muthulakshmi achieved her doctoral degree in Information and Communication Technology from Anna University, Chennai.

Her doctoral research focused on the development of network frameworks in the field of Mobile Ad-Hoc Networks. Her unwavering dedication to engineering innovation is evident through her extensive 25 years of teaching and research experience Dr. K. Muthulakshmi has made a profound impact on the development of network frameworks within academia. She has significantly influenced engineering education by guiding 15 postgraduate projects and 52 undergraduate batches, inspiring the next generation of engineers
